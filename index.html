How To Edit FIGMA

The Canvas in Figma is the backdrop on which all of Frames, Groups, and other Layers live. You can customize your Editor preferences in the View Settings menu. You can find this by clicking the Zoom dropdown in the top-right corner of the Editor: That's all! Enjoy.



The Figma UI allows you to access, create and adjust elements of this design. The sidebar on the Left gives you access to the Layers, Assets and Pages included in your File. The sidebar on the Right allows you to view and adjust the Properties of any object. You can also access the File's Prototype settings and view the Code of any selected object(s).
